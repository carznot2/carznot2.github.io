HELPING DEALERS HELP EACH OTHER!

Instead of sending a unit to the auction you could list it here for trade or sale to another dealer!

Vehicles you have might include over-age inventory, off-make trades that might not fit into your inventory, or specialty commercial vehicles or RVs. The website allows you to list two vehicles on the site at a time. Remember as well, that valuations can differ from one region to another. The website then allows you to take advantage of those differences and you might get a better price from a dealer in another region or across the border, than you would from a customer in your area.

PLEASE NOTE: Our search service is intended for the use of management personnel only and should be used for specific vehicles that a dealer would like to move out of their inventory or trade for a different type of vehicle. It is not intended as a means for dealers to sell retail units at retail prices and should be considered the same as when a dealer sends a vehicle to a wholesale auction where prices less than retail are expected. Typically such units might include overage inventory or off-make vehicles that a dealer might not typically carry on their lot, or for example it might include high mileage pickup trucks or commercial vehicles or RVs that another dealer might have a buyer for.
