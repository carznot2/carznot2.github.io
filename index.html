TESTIMONIAL

I started in the automotive business selling new and used vehicles in Calgary, Alberta in 1975. After having completed a year of university I needed to find a job to go back to school and finish my degree, but being married with a family it quickly became more of a dream than a reality. 48 years later, after having had a successful career selling cars and light trucks and with a clientele that came back to me year after year to purchase vehicles I retired and I went back to school to finish my degree. Back in 1973 I studied accounting, but now I am back I am studying Website Design and Data Management, and with the encouragement of some great individuals including my wife and with 46 years of experience selling cars and trucks I have developed this website to help dealers sell more vehicles.

During those 46 years I sold new Fords, but I also sold almost every other make of vehicle there was, because those were what my customers, who came to see me year after year, sometimes wanted. So when past customers or prospects would phone, instead of telling them that I had nothing like they wanted on the lot I would say that I would check and call them back, and then immediately I would email 30 or 40 other dealers to find out if they could help.

Most often another dealer had what I needed. Then together we would figure out a price and I would phone my customer and tell them what I had, and what they would have to pay for it and because availability is often more of on an issue to the customer than the price I usually made the deal. Such can be verified by the dealerships I used to work for, but for example, in one year (2016), as a New Vehicle Salesperson I sold 92 used vehicles, 70 of which I acquired from other dealers. Other years for me were much the same, but I made a lot of money doing that and the dealerships I worked for did as well.
