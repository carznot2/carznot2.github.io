PAGE IS UNDER CONSTRUCTION

(1) This page contains vehicles that dealers have available to trade.

(2) The information showing includes that of the vehicle only. It does not show who the seller is.

(3) This page can be reached by any user, registered or not.

(4) If the user is not registered this is all that they can see.

(5) If the user is registered, then they can click onto any of the listings and it will display who the seller is and their contact information. It will also show a 'print' button that the user can click. This printed page shows as a worksheet and contains all of the information on the vehicle. This printed page might also enable the user to discuss the vehilce with others on their team, including what they would pay for it, prior to contacting the seller. (6) Pricing is not normally shown on our site, though it might be included in the list in 'other info'. We discourage pricing because the site should be considered much like with an auction, where dealers might negotiate with each other as to what they deem is a fair price. It might also be difficult for a dealer to put a price on a vehicle they are trying to sell if they are not familiar with it. In such examples the listing might give them the opportunity to speak to several dealers so as to establish a fair value.
